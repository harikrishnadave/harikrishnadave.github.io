Prioritization Principles
1
Leverage existing investments first

ServiceNow WSD and Swoogo are already paid for — extract full value before buying anything new.

2
High-impact, low-friction wins

Prioritize what employees feel immediately — faster booking, less app-switching, better wayfinding.

3
AI follows data, not promises

No AI features in Q1. Recommendations launch in Q2 only after a full quarter of real occupancy signal.

4
Contingent workforce parity

20K+ contingent workers need the same seamless experience as FTEs. Inclusion drives platform ROI.
