WealthBridge — Unified Investor Banking
A 4-screen interactive prototype showing how a brokerage end-investor can be seamlessly converted into a full banking customer through behavioral triggers, pre-filled KYC, and a unified financial dashboard. Demonstrates the Clearing-to-Consumer pipeline product concept.
Fintech Consumer Banking Clearing → Consumer AI/Agentic KYC/Identity
Screen 1: Investor's brokerage portal — live holdings, allocation, behavioral milestone trigger
Screen 2: Before/after value prop — fragmented vs. unified, +$876/yr opportunity
Screen 3: One-step onboarding with pre-filled KYC (identity reuse from brokerage)
Screen 4: North-star unified dashboard — banking + investing + AI money routing
